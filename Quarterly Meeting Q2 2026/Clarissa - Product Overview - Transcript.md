### 4) Clarissa’s product expectations and standards

Clarissa outlined expected standards for product practice:

1. **Outcome orientation** over activity reporting.
2. **Customer and business first**—understand the customer problem deeply, not just requested UI changes.
3. **Get out of the building**—in-person customer immersion as a performance expectation.
4. **Speed with accountability**—reduce decision inertia while preserving quality.
5. **Ownership and accountability**—product must define and communicate outcomes clearly so cross-functional teams can execute with context.
6. **Not a feature factory**—focus on value and measurable impact.

She noted she plans to formalize minimum standards and expectations for product roles.
