## Purpose

This artifact is designed for **third-party consumption** — specifically for team members who want clarity on what the onsite is focusing on and for the CTO (Saji) to have a clear, professional view of the theme, strategic context, focus areas, and expected outcomes.

It is **not** a facilitation guide. It contains no internal coaching references, hidden facilitation intent, or operational session logistics. It is a clean, well-designed summary suitable for sharing with leadership stakeholders.

### 2. Strategic Context — Why Now
- Three strategic drivers for this specific quarter
- 2026 OKR targets (EMR retention, AI Scribe, EBITDA, ARR) visualized to show the business stakes

### 3. Leadership Arc
- Compact timeline from Q1 2024 through Q4 2025 showing the intentional development arc
- Q2 2026 positioned as the natural next step, not a restart

### 4. Two-Day Focus Areas
- 10 focus cards, each tagged Day 1, Day 2, or Both
- Guest participation for Dan (Security) and Clarissa (CPO) noted contextually
- No facilitation method, hidden intent, or operational logistics

### 5. Expected Outcomes
- 10 specific deliverables the team is accountable for producing
- Framed as artifacts and decisions, not activities
