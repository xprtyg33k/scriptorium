Executive Summary

The SEL Q2 2026 onsite produced a clear leadership inflection point: the team is moving from directional leadership to measurable ownership. Team feedback was overwhelmingly positive, with strong alignment on stabilizing delivery, supporting Product priorities to regain market trust, and prioritizing outcomes over feature-factory throughput.

Team dynamics improved notably — stronger first-team behavior, lower defensiveness, and clear rejection of blame deflection. Cross-functional communication quality also improved: language that is empathetic, specific, and measurable is producing better alignment and more productive collaboration.

The team aligned on a critical next-stage requirement: value claims must be validated through customer usage evidence, requiring better instrumentation, observation frameworks, and customer-proximate feedback loops. SEL also explicitly acknowledged that Alignment Decay is real and committed to sustained communication cadence to confirm understanding and adoption, not just message transmission.

Leadership Shift

From direction-setting to accountable execution with measurable outcomes.

Strategic Focus

Stability + Product support to rebuild trust and improve outcome delivery.

Operating Posture

Reduced blame, increased ownership, and stronger first-team collaboration.

Execution Discipline

Customer-value proof via instrumentation, observation, and evidence loops.
